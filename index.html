UofT (Freshman)
University of Toronto (UofT) is a globally top-ranked public research university located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Their Computer Science program is currently the top-ranked in Canada, and one of the highest ranked in the world.

Google CSSI (Alumni)
CSSI is an online, four-week introduction to computer science (CS) for rising first year college students with a passion for technology, and seeks to inspire the tech leaders and innovators of tomorrow and build self confidence, agency, and efficacy.

Memofood (Co-Founder)
Memofood is a company whose goal is to reduce food waste by reminding users of their food's expiry dates, decreasing one of the leading causes of food waste in North America.

SHAD (Alumni)
SHAD is a STEAM program for students in Grade 10 and 11. It is a month-long program that prepares students to take on social and economic challenges.

TKS (Alumni)
The Knowledge Society (TKS) is Canada's leading innovation incubator for young leaders age 13-17. TKS develops ambitious young people to impact billions using emerging technologies.

FDS (Alumni)
Future Design School (FDS) empowers students with the skills they need to be ready for the future, while helping hundreds of students in dozens of cities to learn skills in app design.

UCC (Old Boy)
Upper Canada College (UCC) is an IB World School that enrolls 1,160 boys in Senior Kindergarten through Year 12, and has 10,000 alumni in more than 75 countries.
